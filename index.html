⚙ Settings & Sync

Your data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using your passphrase before being stored in Dropbox. Your Dropbox token is also encrypted at rest — it is never stored in plain text.

Dropbox setup: In your App Console → Permissions, ensure files.content.read and files.content.write are checked, then regenerate your token. Add your GitHub Pages URL to CORS origins if needed.
