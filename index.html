⚙ Settings & Dropbox Sync

Data syncs to tasks.json in your Dropbox app folder.

Required one-time setup: In your Dropbox App Console → Settings → OAuth 2, add your GitHub Pages URL (e.g. https://yourusername.github.io) to both Redirect URIs and CORS origins if present.

Then under Permissions, ensure files.content.read and files.content.write are checked — then regenerate your token.
